My Story

Built from
the ground up

Meir Scott didn't stumble into coaching — he was pushed into it by necessity. After a decade of chasing the wrong version of success, he found himself burned out, disconnected, and searching for something real.

That search led him through years of study, mentorship, and the kind of honest self-examination that most people avoid. Today, he's certified, experienced, and deeply committed to helping others shortcut the hard lessons he had to learn himself.

With clients across the UK and beyond, Meir has helped entrepreneurs, executives, athletes, and everyday people reclaim their direction and move forward with confidence.

The philosophy

What Meir
believes

Meir believes every person carries within them an extraordinary capacity for change — but most never access it because they're too busy managing their current story to write a better one.

His coaching is direct, compassionate, and rooted in results. He doesn't do fluff. He doesn't do toxic positivity. He does honest, structured conversations that help you see yourself more clearly and act more decisively.

Influenced by the frameworks of Tony Robbins, James Clear, and Marshall Goldsmith — and grounded in his own hard-won experience — Meir brings a uniquely human approach to the art of transformation.
