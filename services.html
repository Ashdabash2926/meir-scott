FAQ

Common
questions

Coaching works best when you're ready to take responsibility for your results and committed to doing the work. If you're feeling stuck, unclear, or like you're capable of more than you're currently living — that's a good sign. The discovery call will help you figure out if we're the right fit.

Therapy typically focuses on healing past wounds and mental health. Coaching is forward-focused — we start from where you are now and work toward where you want to go. Meir is not a therapist. If you need therapeutic support, he'll be honest about that and may refer you on.

1:1 programmes run for either 3 months (6 sessions) or 6 months (12 sessions). The group programme is 8 weeks. The minimum commitment for any coaching engagement is 3 months — real change takes time, and anything shorter rarely delivers lasting results.

All sessions are conducted via video call (Zoom or Google Meet), which means Meir works with clients across the UK and internationally. In-person sessions in Leeds can be arranged on a case-by-case basis for local clients.
